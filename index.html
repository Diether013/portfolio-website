Design Development

The design development phase executes the schematic design phase and further refines the details that were initially established. For example, the designer finalizes the schematic design that was approved by the client and begins the work on the general structural details for the building. This includes the placement of doors and windows as well as deciding on the materials that will be used in construction. There’s still give and take between the designer and the architect and costs are estimated but changes could still occur.
